Post-Apocalyptic FPS game

For this project, the assignment was to pick a detail from a movie scene from a given list of movies and work out that detail in any way you like. The detail I took from the movie: I Am Legend was that it is post apocalyptic. So I made a post apocalyptic FPS.

The game includes the following mechanics all made with unity and my own coding: Inventory system with pickup included, shooting system, AI, different damage colliders, and a ragdoll system

In the video you can see the bullet impacts. You can also see that the direction is different each time per bullet. They're random.
